Holding Down Notes for Multiple Beats

Sometimes a shabad requires that you hold down a note for multiple beats with pressing the note multiple times. To achieve this in Learn Kirtan, type a dash after the note to hold it down for another beat.

For example, typing "sa" would play Sa once. Typing "sa sa" would play sa twice, but would press the key twice. Typing "sa -" would press the Sa key once but hold it down for two beats.
